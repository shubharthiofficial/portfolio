About Me

Designing clarity for complex enterprise systems.

I design enterprise software that helps people make better decisions in complex systems.

Professional Journey

Over the last 13+ years, I've evolved from UX consulting into enterprise product design, working across telecommunications, finance, healthcare, utilities, and digital transformation initiatives. Today, I focus on designing enterprise platforms where complex business processes become intuitive user experiences.

Current Focus

At Amdocs, I design B2B products across Sales, Commerce, Order Management, and Operations. My work spans UX strategy, information architecture, interaction design, and AI-assisted experiences, delivered through close collaboration with Product, Engineering, Business Architecture, and UI Design.

Design Philosophy

I believe great enterprise design isn't about adding features—it's about reducing complexity. My approach combines systems thinking, structured information architecture, and interaction design to help users make confident decisions within complex business environments.
